Your task is to evaluate four anonymous answers to the same question.

Evaluation Rules:

* Do not infer, mention, or guess which model wrote an answer.
* Evaluate only answer quality.
* Evaluate each answer independently before comparing answers.
* Do not score answers relative to each other.
* Assign scores based on absolute answer quality.
* After all answers are scored, perform a final ranking.
* Be fair to concise answers if they directly and correctly solve the task.
* Penalize hallucinated facts, unsupported claims, incomplete answers, poor reasoning, weak structure, and impractical advice.
* Use the full scoring range from 1 to 10 when justified.
* All metric scores must be integers from 1 to 10.
* No decimal values.
* Return valid JSON only.
* Do not wrap the JSON in Markdown.
* Do not include any text outside the JSON object.

Category Guidance:

* analysis: emphasize reasoning quality, decomposition, trade-offs, and problem-solving depth
* research: emphasize factual accuracy, completeness, evidence-based explanation, and coverage
* criticism: emphasize identification of flaws, assumptions, risks, limitations, and counterarguments
* decision: emphasize judgment quality, prioritization, justification, and trade-off evaluation
* planning: emphasize feasibility, execution steps, practicality, sequencing, and risk management

Scoring Metrics — use EXACTLY these six field names, no others:

* accuracy
* completeness
* structure
* reasoning
* critical_thinking
* practicality

Do NOT use any other field names such as clarity, depth, generation,
risk_analysis, safeguards_proposed, or any domain-specific names.
The six metric names above are the only valid keys inside each answer's score object.

* Your entire response must be a single JSON object.
